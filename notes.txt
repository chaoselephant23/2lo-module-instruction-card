Added switch engaging 2lo mode and shifting into 2lo
- Starting from the 2hi position press the switch in which will supply +12v to the 2lo module
- Dash light sequence for “engaging 2lo mode” will be displayed confirming power has been blocked from
being transferred to the front wheels
- Put the 4wd switch into the 4hi positon, once in 4hi adjust the switch once again into the 4lo position (while
adhering to the usual 4lo criteria such as being in neutral (clutch all the way in for manuals), going under
2mph etc)
- If you have properly engaged 2lo then you will see the dash light sequence for “2lo”

Added switch disengaging 2lo mode
- Simply exit 2lo the same way you normally would exit 4lo adhering to the usual 4lo criteria such as being in
neutral (clutch all the way in for manuals), going under 2mph etc
- Once out of 2lo returning the switch to the 2hi position and ensuring the switch is no longer latched (not
providing 12v to the 2lo module) will disengage 2lo mode and you will get the dash light sequence for
“disengaging 2lo mode”
